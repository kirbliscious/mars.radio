Cyber Arts Camp

Learn cutting edge technology and sustainability from the leading minds in their fields.

The Martians are in the dark and need YOUR help. Beings from across the universe will teleport to your computer and teach you the skills necessary to build a solar-powered light sculpture to illuminate the night sky and free the Martians.

Lessons

  • Learn

    We'll take you on a journey to design, construct, and illuminate your own light sculpture. No prior coding experience necessary. All materials are included in your space kit.

  • Arduino

    Become the master of your universe with the Arduino, a cheap, programmable, open-source microcontroller which makes it easy to control motors, lights, electronics, and many other things.

  • LEDs

    Go from 0 to 255 with RGB. Learn how to address individual LEDs, additive versus subtractive mediums, and explore how rainbows are created through light refraction.

  • Solar Power

    Where we're going, we don't need... outlets. Learn how to harness the power of the sun with our solar power kits.

  • Art

    Cyber Arts Camp coaches are a dynamic group of creative practitioners working at the intersection of art and technology. Students will engage with these topics through creative problem solving.

  • Sculpture

    Let's travel to the third dimension with the help of Buckminster Fuller while exploring geometric and architectural principles.

  • Modularity

    See how to break down a complex structure into its various components, so that you can reconfigure and repurpose them.

  • Sustain

    Sustainability starts with self-reliance. Learn how to build, understand, maintain, and modify each of the components of your sculpture.

  • Create

    Synthesize these concepts together to create a dynamic light sculpture with a unique identity.

We're the Martians

Mars Radio is an educational resource built from the collective knowledge of a cooperative of artists, technologists, and explorers of all kinds. Our knowledge spans across many fields, from sustainable off-grid living to up-and-coming technology. This educational program is an invitation for you to join us, to help us harness these powerful tools to improve your community.
